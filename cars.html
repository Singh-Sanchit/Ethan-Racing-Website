ER 1.0 : Weight - 300 kg's


Team Ethan Racing debuted into the world of Formula Student, participated in 2014 Supra with its first prototype ER 1.0 with 22 members and limited resources .The team successfully managed to come up with a single cylinder open wheel racing car .The team placed 58th in its debue event .The team learned a lot from the experience which pushed our determination and paved our way for our Formula Student life.

CHASSIS

1) AISI 1018 Space Frame.
2) MIG Welding.
2) Wheel Base 1600mm.

SUSPENSION

1) Wheel Base 1600mm.
2) Custom made Adjustable Damper.
3) Designed and Manufactured A-arm's.
4) Wheel Base 1350mm and 1250mm Rear.
5) Continential 185/60/R13.

STEERING

1) Steering Assembly.
2) Rack and Pinion Arrangement with Custom Made Quick Disconnect Arrangement.

BRAKES

1) Custom Rotors.
2) Dual Piston Caliper.
3) Custom made and Manufacturing Pedal Box Assembly.

ENGINE

1)Royal Enfield Bullet 350.
2)Displacement 346cc.
3)Direct Throttle.

DRIVETRAIN

1)Sequential Speed Transmission Integrated in Engine Casting.
